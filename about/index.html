About Dargarth

After a long hiatus, we're baaaack!!! We’re starting with a soft opening with no lasting land actions.

We’re a full-contact, sufficient-force wargame LARP based in Seattle, founded in 2010. Dargarth began as a cross between the in-depth roleplaying wargame Darkon and the combat only boffer group Belegarth. We’ve since evolved independently with diverse stories and history.
